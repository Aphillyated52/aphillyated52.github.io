Public toilets in Travemünde

The short answer: in and around Travemünde there are 19 publicly usable toilets – 15 public facilities and 4 “Nette Toilette” businesses whose WC you may use without buying anything. The key points on a beach day: the facilities at the Kurstrand (DLRG main station, Brügmanngarten), on the Vorderreihe in the town centre and on the Priwall (ferry landing, south mole and Waldweg); two facilities also stand along the Brodten shore path. The map and full list with all locations and accessibility notes follow below – the data source is the Hanseatic City of Lübeck's open data.

Frequently asked questions

Where is the nearest toilet at the Kurstrand?

Right by the beach: the public facility at the DLRG main station (Kaiserallee 6) and the one in the Brügmanngarten; both are in the list on this page with full details.

Are there public toilets on the Priwall?

Yes, several: at the ferry landing, the south mole, twice along the Waldweg and in the holiday-village area – all locations are on the map on this page.

What is a “Nette Toilette”?

Participating cafés and restaurants make their WC available for free, no purchase needed – recognisable by the “Nette Toilette” sticker. Several Travemünde businesses take part; they are marked in the list.

Where are accessible toilets?

Several facilities are accessible (marked in the list); the fully accessible ones use the Euro toilet key. All details are on the accessibility page.
