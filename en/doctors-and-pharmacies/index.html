Help, fast

Which emergency numbers apply in Travemünde?

In an acute, life-threatening situation every second counts – call 112 first. The other numbers help outside surgery hours when it is not an emergency.

Emergency call

  • 112

    Ambulance and fire service

    Life-threatening emergencies: heart attack, stroke, serious accidents, breathing difficulty, unconsciousness.

    Call 112
  • 110

    Police emergency

    For danger, crime or accidents when police are needed on site.

    Call 110
  • 116117

    Out-of-hours medical service

    When it cannot wait for surgery hours but is not a 112 emergency – evenings, nights, weekends. Nationwide, free of charge.

    Call 116117

In Travemünde itself the Sana day clinic (Am Dreilingsberg 7) handles surgical and general emergencies – hours vary, call ahead. The nearest full 24-hour A&E departments are UKSH Campus Lübeck and the Sana Kliniken Lübeck, each about 15–20 km away.

Common questions

Which pharmacy is on emergency duty in Travemünde today?

The pharmacy emergency service rotates daily – there is no fixed emergency pharmacy. The pharmacy on duty today for 23570 Travemünde is shown on aponet.de, or you can call the free line 0800 0022833 (from a mobile: 22833).

When do I call 112 and when 116117?

112 is for life-threatening emergencies such as heart attack, stroke, serious accidents or breathing difficulty. 116117 is the out-of-hours medical service for complaints that cannot wait for surgery hours but are not an emergency – evenings, nights and weekends.

Is there an A&E in Travemünde?

The Sana day clinic in Travemünde (Am Dreilingsberg 7) handles surgical and general emergencies on site; hours change, so call ahead. Full 24-hour A&E departments are at UKSH Campus Lübeck and the Sana Kliniken Lübeck, each about 15–20 kilometres away.

Which GPs are there in Travemünde?

Travemünde has, among others, the Hausarztpraxis im Hafenhaus, the Allgemeinarztpraxis Pier 3, the Praxis im Moorredder and the Praxis am Dreilingsberg. Addresses and phone numbers are in the directory on this page.

Where do I find the dental emergency service?

The dental emergency service for the region on weekends and public holidays is provided by the statewide Schleswig-Holstein duty service at zahnaerztlicher-notdienst-sh.de.
