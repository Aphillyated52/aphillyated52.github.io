Good to know – what is no longer there

  • There is no cinema in Travemünde – movie night means a trip to Lübeck.
  • Classic mini golf disappeared from town in 2013; today you play adventure golf on the Priwall and blacklight mini golf indoors. The city is planning a new course in the Brügmanngarten “Sportgarten” (planning resolution January 2026).
  • The former casino closed in 2012 – the building now houses the ATLANTIC Grand Hotel.
  • The angling boat MS Karoline now sails from Fehmarn, no longer from Niendorf.

Sources: HL-live (Sportgarten, January 2026), operator information – as of 10 June 2026.

Seasonal in the winter half-year

Once the beach chairs are in storage: the “SCHIFF AHOI” Christmas market (late November to early January), the winter world in the Brügmanngarten, beach rides from the riding stable (15 September to 1 May) and the ice-bathing season, opened by the traditional first swim. There is no ice rink in Travemünde – but there is the Eiswelt in Scharbeutz.
