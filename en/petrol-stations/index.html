And on the Priwall?

On the Priwall there is a boat fuel station in the marina area for water-sports – it is not a car station. To refuel a car, use the stations in town or in Kücknitz.

Frequently asked questions about petrol stations in Travemünde

Which petrol stations are there in Travemünde?

In the town itself there is an Aral on Gneversdorfer Weg (24 hours) and a TotalEnergies on Moorredder (until 10 p.m.). On the way via Kücknitz there is a Shell on Borstelweg (24 hours).

Is there a 24-hour petrol station in Travemünde?

Yes, the Aral on Gneversdorfer Weg and the Shell in Kücknitz are open around the clock.

Where is the cheapest place to refuel in Travemünde?

Fuel prices change several times a day. This page shows – where available – the current prices of the stations, so comparing diesel, Super and E10 pays off.

Is there a petrol station on the Priwall?

Only a boat fuel station in the marina area for water-sports. There is no car station on the Priwall – use the stations in town or in Kücknitz.
