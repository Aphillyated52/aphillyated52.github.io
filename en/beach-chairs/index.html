Common questions about beach chairs

How do you book a beach chair in Travemünde?

Many Travemünde beach chairs can be reserved online via Strandbutler – pick a section and your days. Alternatively rent on the spot at the operators’ stands while chairs last. On summer weekends and during Travemünde Week (17–26 July), book days ahead.

Which beach chair operators are there in Travemünde?

In Travemünde, 8 operators rent beach chairs on the Kurstrand and the Priwall – most stands are along the beach promenade. Phone, opening hours and Google listing are in the list above.

Is there a sleeping beach chair in Travemünde?

Yes. Seipel offers a sleeping beach chair – an overnight stay right on the beach. The listing is reachable via the Google Maps link in the operator list.

Are there accessible beach chairs in Travemünde?

Yes. Kanthak offers accessible chairs with a turntable, and Seipel lends free beach swimming wheelchairs. The official accessibility overview is on travemuende-tourismus.de.
