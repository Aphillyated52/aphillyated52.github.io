How to get your chair

01

Reserve online

Many Travemünde chairs can be booked online – pick a section and your days. Travemünde is listed on Strandbutler.

strandbutler.de ↗
02

Rent on the spot

Day rental at the operators’ stands while chairs last – come early on summer weekends, the front row goes first.

03

Special chairs

Free beach swimming wheelchairs from Seipel, accessible chairs with turntable from Kanthak – and for a night by the sea, Seipel’s sleeping beach chair (in the list above).

Official accessibility overview ↗

Good to know

  • Seasonal: off season the chairs are in winter storage.
  • For Travemünde Week (17–26 July) and hot weekends, reserve days ahead.
  • The resort fee is separate from the beach chair – details on the beach page.
  • Prices are set by the operators – at the stand, in their Google listing and in online booking. This page invents no numbers.

Sources: travemuende-tourismus.de (accessibility page, retrieved 11 June 2026) · strandbutler.de · research of 10 June 2026. Verified 11 June 2026.
