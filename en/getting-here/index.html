Across the Trave

Priwall ferry

The municipal ferry shuttles between the Vorderreihe and the Priwall – for passengers and cars, at short intervals during the day. Special times apply during Travemünde Week.

No ferry disruption reported · As of 18 June 2026, 08:35

By car: parking

Five car parks sit close to the beach; for the town centre and the ferry scene, use the Hafenbahnhof car park. There are no live occupancy figures – on summer weekends, arrive early or take the train right to the beach.
