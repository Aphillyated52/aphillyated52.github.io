Common questions about getting here

How do I get to Travemünde?

By train from Lübeck (a stop right by the beach: Lübeck-Travemünde Strand), by car via the A1 and B75, by the Priwall ferry across the Trave, or by the Finnlines and TT-Line ferries from Scandinavia.

Where can I park in Travemünde?

Several car parks lie close to the beach, plus the Hafenbahnhof car park for the town centre and ferry area. There are currently no live occupancy figures; on summer weekends, arrive early or come by train.

Does the train stop in Travemünde?

Yes, at three stops: Lübeck-Travemünde Strand (closest to beach and promenade), Lübeck-Travemünde Hafen and Lübeck-Skandinavienkai. Live departure times are at the top of this page.

Is there a ferry to Scandinavia from Travemünde?

Yes, from Skandinavienkai. Finnlines and TT-Line operate routes to Scandinavia from Travemünde; timetables and booking directly with each operator (links on this page).

How do I get from Travemünde to the Priwall?

By the Priwall ferry between the Vorderreihe and the Priwall – as a passenger and car ferry. The current disruption status is shown at the top of this page.
