    aja Travemünde – SEEBAD Day-Spa

Good to know

  • Day spa means: pool, sauna and treatments can be used without an overnight stay – partly in fixed time slots (such as an early bird in the morning or in the evening). Capacities are limited, so booking pays off.
  • The sauna highlight in town is the view of the water: from the Finnish sauna at the aja you look out over the Baltic and beach.
  • We deliberately do not quote fixed prices or current times – they change seasonally and are with each venue. Please check and book there.

Frequently asked questions about wellness in Travemünde

Can you use a spa in Travemünde without staying overnight?

Yes. Both the SEEBAD spa at the aja Travemünde and the beauty and spa at the ATLANTIC Grand Hotel offer a day spa – pool, sauna and treatments for day guests too. Capacities are limited, so book in advance.

Where is there a sauna with a sea view in Travemünde?

At the aja Travemünde: from the Finnish sauna the view reaches over the Baltic and the beach. The ATLANTIC Grand Hotel has a spa of around 600 m² with a Finnish sauna, sanarium and two steam baths.

What does a day spa in Travemünde cost?

Prices differ by venue and time slot and change seasonally. We deliberately quote no fixed amounts – current conditions are directly with the aja or the ATLANTIC Grand Hotel.
