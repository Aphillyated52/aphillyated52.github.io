What does parking cost in Travemünde?

Fees depend on the parking zone. Central, beach-side spaces (Mövenstein, Leuchtenfeld, ferry harbour) are in the more expensive Zone IV, edge areas in Zone V. Charges apply daily from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m.

  • Zone IV (central: Mövenstein, Leuchtenfeld, ferry harbour) 30 min 1,60 € · up to 3,20 €/h · day ticket 12 €
  • Zone V (edge area) 30 min 1,20 € · no day ticket

Fees since 1 June 2025 (Hansestadt Lübeck / KWL). Check the full zone table and current tariffs at parken-luebeck.de.

Free parking: the Kowitzberg car park

At Kowitzberg on the edge of town you park free of charge – around 450 spaces, no ticket, no parking disc and no time limit. It is about a 15-minute walk to the centre, or take a bus. It is the cheapest choice for a full beach day.

Motorhomes and EVs

Public motorhome pitches cost 20,00 € per 24 hours; an overnight stay is permitted only once to restore fitness to drive, not for long-term parking. Fully electric vehicles and plug-in hybrids with an E number plate park free until 31 December 2026 – but only in Zones II, III and V, not in the central Zone IV.

How do you pay?

Pay at the ticket machine or by mobile parking. Accepted apps include PayByPhone and mobilet – enter the parking-space number shown on the machine. The operator is KWL (Kommunale Wirtschaftsbetriebe Lübeck).

Frequently asked questions about parking in Travemünde

What does parking cost in Travemünde?

In the central, beach-side Zone IV, 30 minutes cost 1,60 €, an hour up to 3,20 €, a day ticket 12 €. In edge Zone V, 30 minutes are 1,20 € (no day ticket). Charges apply daily from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. (in force since 1 June 2025).

Where can you park for free in Travemünde?

At the Kowitzberg car park on the edge of town – around 450 spaces, free of charge, no ticket and no time limit. It is about a 15-minute walk to the centre.

Can you park a motorhome in Travemünde?

Yes, at public motorhome pitches for 20,00 € per 24 hours. Staying overnight is permitted only once to restore fitness to drive, not for long-term parking.

Do EVs park for free in Travemünde?

Fully electric vehicles and plug-in hybrids with an E number plate park free until 31 December 2026 – but only in Zones II, III and V, not the central Zone IV.

How do you pay for parking in Travemünde?

At the ticket machine or via a mobile-parking app (incl. PayByPhone, mobilet). In the app, enter the parking-space number shown on the machine.
