Fresh fish from the cutter

The fishing harbour on the Baggersand is the culinary heart of the resort: the cutters usually return to their berths around 8 a.m., and the freshly caught fish is sold straight from the boat or at stalls on land. It doesn’t get fresher – to experience the bustle, come in the morning. There are no fixed sales times; it depends on the catch and the weather.

More than fish

Travemünde is not only fish: along the Vorderreihe and the promenade there are cafés, ice-cream parlours and restaurants from Italian to regional; on the Priwall the BeachBay market hall invites you in with delicatessen. Good to know

  • The fish is freshest in the morning at the fishing harbour, shortly after the cutters return (usually around 8 a.m.). There are no fixed sales times.
  • Around the nationwide World Fish Roll Day (first Saturday in May) the coast holds special events – a good reason to visit.
  • We list known, verified addresses and deliberately avoid invented stars or “best-of” rankings. The order is not a rating.

Frequently asked questions about eating in Travemünde

Where are the best fish rolls in Travemünde?

Well-known addresses are Königs Fischbrötchen and Fisch Stengel at the fishing harbour, Fischkombüse on the Vorderreihe and at the harbour, Gosch with a Trave view and the Fischerkajüte on the Priwall. “Best” is a matter of taste – we don’t invent a ranking.

Where can I get fresh fish straight from the cutter in Travemünde?

At the fishing harbour on the Baggersand: the cutters usually return around 8 a.m. and the catch is sold straight from the boat or at stalls on land. There are no fixed times – the morning is the best chance.

Are there restaurants in Travemünde beyond fish?

Yes, along the Vorderreihe, the promenade and on the Priwall there are cafés, Italian and international restaurants and ice-cream parlours. The full, data-backed directory is on the places page.

When is World Fish Roll Day?

World Fish Roll Day takes place nationwide on the first Saturday in May; the Baltic coast holds special events around fresh fish.
